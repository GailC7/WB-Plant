Lawn & Garden Tools

Here you can find useful information about gardening tools/accessories and what they're used for.

Get started growing your garden.

Tools

Garden Trowels

Garden Trowels are used for digging small holes, in order to plant bulbs, annuals or perennials. These wouldn't be used for digging a tree or shrub. Trowels can also be used for weeding!

Garden Rakes

Rakes are used in gardening in order to scoop, scrape or gather. Other rakes can be used as a tool for leveling materials like soil, mulch, or leaves. Some rakes have flat heads, most have sharp metal tines used to break up compacted soil or rock.

Garden Hoes

Garden Hoes are used for cultivating soil and removing weeds. Hoes actually predate the plow and has been used for millenia when it comes to cultivating soil for plants.

Weeders

There are many different types of weeding tools, all specifically designed to remove weeds from your lawn or elsewhere. Cape Cod weeders are used in tighter spaces, while a paving weeder can be used for getting rid of weeds growing between or on concrete slabs.

Shovels

Shovels are used for removing large amounts of soil. They are meant for digging, transplanting, and lifting for all sorts of garden tasks.

Garden Gloves

Garden Gloves are used to keep your hands safe from injury, while also protecting you from the dirt, grime, and on-hands tasks you will perform in your garden.

Carts & Wheelbarrows

Wheelbarrows are used for transferring materials often too heavy to carry by yourself. They can be used for moving rock, soil, mulch, certain trees and shrubs, as well as mixing concrete or fertilizer.
